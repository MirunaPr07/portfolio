About

Welcome to my portfolio! First of all, a few words about me: I am a Computer Science student who genuinely enjoys learning, taking challenges and pushing my logic as far as possible. I like problems that make me think, especially the ones that don't have an obvious solution from the start.

Technically speaking, I enjoy writing algorithms most of all; what I love is analizing the project, coming up with the idea and only after that, writing the code. I like searching for patterns, analizing the limit cases and only then translating my solution into something structured and clean. I am particularly drawn to low-level programming and data structures, but I also enjoy working with Java and web technologies. I appreciate projects that "play with my brain" and force me to approach things from different angles.

I built this website to bring together the projects I cared about the most during university, the ones I worked on with real curiosity and involvement. I organized them by category so it's easier to navigate through the different areas I explored.

More than anything, this portfolio reflects how I learn: once I understand the problem, I adapt quickly and improve through practice. I believe the variety of projects here shows my versatility and how comfortably I can move between different types of systems, from algorithms and low-level logic to distributed systems, graphics or application development.
